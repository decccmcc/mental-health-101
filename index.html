Understanding Mental Health

Mental health encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Good mental health is crucial at every stage of life, from childhood to adulthood. Learn more about the signs of mental health issues and how to seek help.

Why Mental Health Matters

Just like physical health, our mental health changes over time and is shaped by many things. It influences how we handle stress, connect with others, and make decisions.

Common Factors That Affect Mental Well-Being:

  • Life events: Experiences like stress, trauma, or major change can have lasting effects.
  • Relationships: Support from family, friends, or community helps build resilience.
  • Physical health: Sleep, nutrition, and regular activity all influence how we feel.
  • Environment: Our surroundings — home, work, and community — impact our sense of safety and stability.

Recognizing these influences helps us understand why mental health is important — and why caring for it is a lifelong process.

Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you maintain good mental health:

  • Stay connected with friends and family.
  • Engage in regular physical activity.
  • Practice mindfulness and relaxation techniques.
  • Seek professional help when needed.
